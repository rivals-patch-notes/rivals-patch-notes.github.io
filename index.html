Some heroes look buffed in the raw patch notes but aren't. Invisible Woman's Orb Projection gained 5 healing (45→50), yet with her removed anchor boost it used to heal for an effective 51.75, so the "buff" is really a net nerf. Everything below shows the net change after anchor removal: what each hero truly gained or lost.

"All / Other" chips are the abilities the patch left untouched. They lose the full anchor bonus.
